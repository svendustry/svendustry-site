The operator

One person. Many businesses.

Built by Sven, run by the Great Hall

Svendustry is the operating name for an experiment in what one entrepreneur can build when AI does the heavy lifting. Not a team. Not a funded startup. One person, a fleet of agents, and a deliberate system.

The vending business came first — real machines, real locations, real customers in the West Valley Phoenix area. The AI system was built to run the operations, then expanded to build new ones.

The Great Hall is the result — 12 specialized agents working in parallel, governed by a manager, constrained by hard rules, and pointed at real revenue opportunities.
